Recipes

Biscuit

Flour, butter, leavening, salt, sugar, buttermilk.

Keep the butter cold, sift together your soul's dryness. Cut into it with thumbs pressing against index fingers. Rub rhythmatically. Gently fold the angelic dough with buttermilk. Move quickly, keep the chill running down your spine. Use a sharp object. Bake at high heat. Eat when golden and flakey to your taste.

Cinnamon Roll

Sweet potatoes, flour, brown butter, sugar, brown sugar, salt, spices, yeast, milk, eggs, and tears.

Put it all in the bowl, except the butter. Turn it on. Keep the hook spiraling on the lowest speed. Don't take your eyes off the transformation. Watch for smoothness and that intuition kicking in. Add the cold butter slowly. Let rise, fold down, let rise again. Smooth, smear, roll, and slice. Let rise up, bake, frost, and don't let anyone put you down.
Rise! Resist! Roll!
